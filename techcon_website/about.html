History

TechCon started as a small local meetup in Silicon Valley in 1998, founded by a group of tech enthusiasts passionate about innovation and collaboration.

Over the years, it has grown into one of the leading global tech conferences, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning in the tech industry.

We aim to provide a platform where tech professionals can exchange ideas, showcase innovations, and discuss the future of technology.
